## Detailed Signal Explanations

### Signal 1: Institutional Launchpad

**Purpose**: Identifies high-probability entry points where institutional activity aligns with technical breakouts.

**Conditions (Bullish)**:
- Price is within a bullish Order Block
- Consensus score >= 7 (strong bullish momentum)
- Trendline breakout OR pattern breakout detected
- Market structure is bullish (trend = 1)
- Volume confirmation present
- All conflict resolution rules pass

**Conditions (Bearish)**:
- Price is within a bearish Order Block
- Consensus score <= 2 (strong bearish momentum)
- Trendline breakdown OR pattern breakdown detected
- Market structure is bearish (trend = -1)
- Volume confirmation present
- All conflict resolution rules pass

**Trading Action**: This is a high-conviction entry signal. Consider full position size.

**Risk Management**: Place stop loss below the Order Block (long) or above (short).

### Signal 2: Liquidity Trap Reversal

**Purpose**: Captures reversal opportunities after liquidity sweeps where smart money has trapped retail traders.

**Conditions (Bullish)**:
- Bullish sweep detected (price went below previous low then closed above)
- Price is within a bullish Order Block
- Volume confirmation present
- Market structure is bullish
- All conflict resolution rules pass

**Conditions (Bearish)**:
- Bearish sweep detected (price went above previous high then closed below)
- Price is within a bearish Order Block
- Volume confirmation present
- Market structure is bearish
- All conflict resolution rules pass

**Trading Action**: Enter on the reversal candle close. This signal often precedes strong moves.

**Risk Management**: Stop loss below the sweep low (long) or above sweep high (short).

---

### Signal 3: Momentum Breakout

**Purpose**: Identifies breakout opportunities with strong momentum confirmation from the consensus engine.

**Conditions (Bullish)**:
- Trendline breakout detected (close above resistance trendline)
- Consensus score >= 7
- Volume confirmation present
- All conflict resolution rules pass

**Conditions (Bearish)**:
- Trendline breakdown detected (close below support trendline)
- Consensus score <= 2
- Volume confirmation present
- All conflict resolution rules pass

**Trading Action**: Enter on breakout confirmation. Wait for candle close above/below trendline.

**Risk Management**: Stop loss below the breakout candle or recent swing low/high.

---

### Signal 4: Mitigation Test Entry

**Purpose**: Identifies safe re-entry opportunities when price retests an Order Block after initial move.

**Conditions (Bullish)**:
- Price is within a bullish Order Block
- Not a newly formed Order Block (retest scenario)
- Current candle is bullish (close > open)
- Volume confirmation present
- Market structure is bullish
- All conflict resolution rules pass

**Conditions (Bearish)**:
- Price is within a bearish Order Block
- Not a newly formed Order Block (retest scenario)
- Current candle is bearish (close < open)
- Volume confirmation present
- Market structure is bearish
- All conflict resolution rules pass

**Trading Action**: This is a lower-risk entry for adding to positions or entering missed moves.

**Risk Management**: Stop loss at Order Block distal (opposite edge).

### Signal 5: Bullish Exit

**Purpose**: Signals when to exit long positions to protect profits or limit losses.

**Conditions**:
- Active long trade exists AND one of:
  - Price reached Take Profit 1 level
  - Price entered a bearish Order Block (potential reversal zone)
  - Consensus score dropped to 3 or below (momentum exhaustion)

**Trading Action**: Close long position or move stop to breakeven.

**Note**: This signal helps avoid giving back profits during reversals.

  - Price reached Take Profit 1 level

  - Consensus score rose to 6 or above (momentum exhaustion)

### Signal 7: Golden Pocket Flip

**Purpose**: Identifies trend reversal opportunities at key Fibonacci retracement levels.

**Conditions (Bullish)**:
- Structure break detected (CHoCH or BOS)
- Price is in the "golden pocket" zone (0.618-0.786 Fibonacci retracement)
- Price is within a bullish Order Block
- Volume confirmation present
- All conflict resolution rules pass

**Conditions (Bearish)**:
- Structure break detected (CHoCH or BOS)
- Price is in the inverse golden pocket zone (0.214-0.382 Fibonacci retracement)
- Price is within a bearish Order Block
- Volume confirmation present
- All conflict resolution rules pass

**Trading Action**: This signal indicates potential trend reversal. Enter with confirmation.

**Risk Management**: Stop loss beyond the Fibonacci level or Order Block.

### Signal 8: Volatility Squeeze Alert

**Purpose**: Warns of impending large price movement after volatility compression.

**Conditions**:
- ATR is decreasing (current ATR < 70% of previous ATR for 2 consecutive bars)
- Volume is low (< 50% of 20-period average)
- Price range is compressed (20-bar range < 2x ATR)
- Consensus score is neutral (4-5)

**Trading Action**: This is a WARNING signal, not an entry signal. Prepare for breakout in either direction.

**Usage**: 
- Tighten stops on existing positions
- Prepare entry orders above/below the compression zone
- Watch for directional breakout confirmation
